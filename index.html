The diversity of Brazilian gastronomy in one place

If you already love Brazilian barbecue, now you have the opportunity to explore everything else that Brazil has to offer you when it comes to food. We import original ingredients from all 5 regions of Brazil to give you an authentic experience of Feijoada, Baião de Dois, Acarajé, Moqueca, Tacacá and much more.

You are going to love our drink menu too

Here, you can try alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages with the Brazilian taste. You can choose from a wide variety of natural fruits for your juice or Caipirinha. If you enjoy tropical fruits, such as mango, guava, cashew and açaí, come to the Brazilian Cuisine to try our delicious drinks.

Music is part of the experience

Every Saturday, we offer concerts with real Brazilian artists during lunch and dinner times. You will have the pleasure of having your meals listening to samba, pagode, MPB and forró. Do not miss the opportunity to connect to the sounds of Brazil at the Brazilian Cuisine.
